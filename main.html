PLANTS Doctor


Problems in plant paradise? Don’t worry, the plant doctor is here to help!





falling leaves


This can be a sign of under watering or overwatering. Check the instructions that came with the plant to learn how often your plant needs to be watered.





hanging leaves


Usually a sign of an under watered plant or roots that have become overly dry. Consider watering your plant more regularly to bring it back to life.





spot on leaves


Spots on your houseplants’ leaves are often caused when water droplets land on leaves. To prevent these spots from appearing you should water soil.
